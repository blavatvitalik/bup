Its best-known products are the digital distribution service for games and software Steam, PC games and the Half-Life, Portal, Counter-Strike, Left 4 Dead and Dota 2 series. Most of the games developed by Valve use its own GoldSrc, Source and Source 2 game engines. The company is headquartered in Bellevue, Washington, near Seattle.
